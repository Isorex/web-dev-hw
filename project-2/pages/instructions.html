Painting

Step 1 Add 1c of each paint (a colored substance that is spread over a surface and dries to leave a thin decorative or protective coating.) in seperate containers
Step 2 Lay down plastic to prevent paint from getting everywhere
Step 3 Stretch out your canvas or what ever you are painting on
Step 4 Make sure your hands are clean and start with dark colors and work your way up to the light ones
Step 5 Clean your hands between colors
Step 6 Let your painting dry before moving it
Step 7 Show all your friends
